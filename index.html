Picture this.

You're 10 years old. You have made the mature choice to leave home forever to wander the world to tame animals and make them fight for sport. Thankfully, you are family friends with an unscrupulous geneticist who happens to have three endangered animals under his care. He allows you to choose one to begin your bloody conquest, while provoking his grandson, your childhood friend, into a bitter rivalry with you. The choice can be daunting at first, so here's a handy reference if you're not sure which one to pick.

Bulbasaur grows the fastest and is the most effective against the two starting gyms, yet tends not to be as strong later on in the game, even after evolving into the Solar-Beam-capable Venusaur.

Charmander faces a stiff uphill battle at first, particularly ineffective against the first few gyms, and has the steepest growth curve; however, it evolves into the phenomenally powerful Charizard at later levels.

Squirtle is a cute turtle that evolves into an armored, amphibious, artillery-toting commando, and is the clear and obvious choice for any aspiring Pokemon trainer. None can withstand its charisma and might.

Don't stress about it too much. Sure, you may not ever be able to return home, and you'll now have to provide for yourself 100% at the ripe old age of 10, but hey, you gotta grow up sometime!
